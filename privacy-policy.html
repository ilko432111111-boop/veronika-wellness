Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

This privacy policy explains how Veronika Wellness & Aesthetics uses personal information when you contact us through our website, our AI receptionist, Instagram messages, or other business communication channels.

Information we may collect

When you contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number.
  • Your chat messages and enquiry details.
  • The treatment you are interested in.
  • Your preferred appointment date and time.
  • Information needed to respond to your Instagram messages, such as the account identifier provided by Instagram.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Answer questions about treatments, prices, location, and availability.
  • Check whether a requested time currently appears available.
  • Send your booking request to the therapist for review.
  • Contact you to confirm, change, or follow up on your request.
  • Review chat transcripts so we can improve customer service.

AI receptionist

Our AI receptionist helps answer enquiries and collect booking-request details. It does not confirm appointments automatically. Final confirmation is provided by the therapist.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted service providers to operate the website, store messages securely, send notifications, and process chatbot responses. These providers only receive the information needed to provide their service.

How long we keep information

We keep enquiry information only for as long as reasonably needed to respond to your request, manage follow-up, improve customer service, and meet any legal obligations.

Your rights

You may ask us to correct or delete your personal information, or ask what information we hold about you, subject to applicable legal requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact Veronika Wellness & Aesthetics by phone: 07386 396139.
